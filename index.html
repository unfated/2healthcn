Vision

Everyone should be able to foresee disease and safeguard their health. We turn expensive, scattered and expert-gated health data into understandable, actionable, lifelong decision support — for every person.

Mission

Build a full-life-cycle Human Digital Twin for every person, and move healthcare from after-the-fact treatment toward prediction and proactive prevention.

The problem

Health data is expensive, fragmented — and wasted.

People and health systems generate more data than anyone can use. Today it sits in silos, read once and forgotten.

Costly omics, shelved

DNA and multi-omics reports cost $100–1,000 a sample, then sit in PDFs — read once and never used again.

Siloed & fragmented

Check-ups, test reports, medical records and wearables live in separate systems. No one — not even a family doctor — sees a lifelong view.

Generic & low-trust

Direct-to-consumer reports cover a narrow scope with commonsense advice. Those who care about health pay more, yet have no access to reliable, broad insight.

Why 2Health AI

The core AI engine for Digital Public Infrastructure of Health.

Across much of Asia and Africa there is no unified health identity or interoperable record — care cannot follow the patient across hospitals or borders. 2Health AI provides the missing layer: a portable, user-owned Human Digital Twin, and the AI operating system that powers it.

Higher accuracy

Multi-omics prediction built on polygenic risk scores lifts accuracy +30–60% over traditional models — reaching up to 0.9 AUC on key diseases (internal testing).

Broader coverage

A single upload analyses 1,000+ phenotypes across 30+ disease categories — where typical direct-to-consumer reports cover fewer than 100.

Explainable by design

Every genetic risk is visualised against a reference population and interpreted live by an AI assistant — not a static PDF.

Trust, privacy & compliance

Built to be trusted with the most personal data there is.

Decision support, not diagnosis

2HAI is positioned as health information and clinical decision support — not a diagnostic device or prescription. AI guidance is designed to be validated against current clinical guidelines.

Privacy by design

Encryption, de-identification, tiered and user-controlled authorization, and localized storage. Users keep the right to authorize — and revoke — access to their data.

Multi-jurisdiction compliance

Aligned with mainland China's PIPL, Data Security Law and human genetic-resources rules, Hong Kong's PDPO, Singapore's PDPA, and GDPR for international markets.

Proprietary methodology & IP

Self-developed statistical-genetics and polygenic-scoring methods, with 2Health AI trademarks registered or pending across key markets.
