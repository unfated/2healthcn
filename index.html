Who we are

The intelligence layer between health data and human decisions.

Modern medicine generates more data than any team can read. 2Health AI turns that data into clarity — pairing clinical expertise with state-of-the-art AI so clinicians can see sooner, decide faster, and treat with confidence. We are a Hong Kong company, building for care teams and patients across the region and beyond.

Evidence-led

Every model is measured against clinical benchmarks, and every output can be traced back to its source.

What we build

One platform, from signal to decision.

The 2Health AI platform brings clinical intelligence into every step of care — available to explore at 2health.ai.

Clinical decision support

Context-aware guidance at the point of care, grounded in current medical evidence and always explainable.

Health record intelligence

Structure and summarise scattered records into one clear clinical picture in seconds, not minutes.

Early risk detection

Patient triage & navigation
